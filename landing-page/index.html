Why Idemy?

VLC is for movies. Idemy is for learning.

Automatic Parsing

Just select a root folder. Idemy instantly scans your directories, grouping sub-folders as sections and automatically sorting videos by their file names.

Granular Tracking

Your progress is saved locally every 5 seconds. Close the app and resume exactly where you left off. Watch 90% of a video to automatically mark it as complete.

Absolute Privacy

Powered by an ultra-fast local SQLite database. No internet required. No tracking. No cloud syncing. Your data never leaves your hard drive.

Knowledge Base

Timestamp-locked Markdown Notes.

Don't just passively watch. Press N to automatically pause the video and drop a markdown-formatted note exactly at the current timestamp.

Later, clicking the timestamp on any saved note instantly seeks the video back to that exact moment. It's the ultimate reference tool.
